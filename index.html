CoinSim AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

CoinSim AI is a virtual trading simulator for educational purposes only.
No real cryptocurrency is bought, sold, or traded. No real money or digital assets are involved.

Information We Collect

CoinSim AI does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All simulation data (virtual balances, trade history, strategy settings) is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

The app connects to public cryptocurrency market data feeds (e.g., Coinbase WebSocket) solely to display real-time price information for simulation purposes. No user data is sent to these services.

Financial Disclaimer

This app does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or real trading services. All trading activities within the app are entirely simulated with virtual currency. Past simulated performance does not guarantee future results. Consult a qualified financial advisor before making any real investment decisions.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
